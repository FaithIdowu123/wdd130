History

White Water Rafting was founded with one simple goal: to share the exhilaration and beauty of river rafting with the world. What began as a small, passionate group of river guides has grown into a trusted adventure company known for safety, professionalism, and a deep love for the great outdoors.

With decades of combined experience, our team has led thousands of successful trips through some of the most scenic and challenging rivers in the region. From calm scenic floats to heart-pounding Class V rapids, we've crafted adventures for every level of rafter.
